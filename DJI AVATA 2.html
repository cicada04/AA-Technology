DJI AVATA 2

Lightweight design with a 4K camera, 18-minute flight time, and advanced stabilization for a superior FPV experience.

DJI AVATA 2 SPECIFICATION

Video Transmission

DJI’s O4 transmission system offers enhanced stability with a range of up to 13 km and a max transmission bitrate of 60 Mbps

EASY ACRO

The Easy ACRO mode on the DJI Avata 2 simplifies advanced FPV tricks like flips and rolls, making them accessible to beginners. It combines these moves with stabilization for smoother footage and is easy to activate in the DJI Fly app

IMMERSIVE EXPERIENCE

The DJI Avata 2 provides an immersive FPV experience with 1080p goggles, OcuSync 4 for smooth video, and a Motion Controller that tracks hand movements for intuitive flight. Features like pass-through mode allow users to see their surroundings without removing the goggles

SUPER_WIDE 4K SHORTS

The DJI Avata 2 captures 4K video with a 78° field of view for cinematic shots and includes a 115° ultra-wide option for even broader, more immersive FPV experiences. Both angles provide exceptional video clarity, enhanced by RockSteady stabilization for smooth, dynamic footage​
