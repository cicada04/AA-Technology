SPECIFICATIONS

Light Weight 249 g

Light Weight

The DJI Mini 4 Pro weighs only 249 grams, making it ultra-portable and exempt from registration requirements in many regions. Despite its lightweight design, it offers impressive features like advanced camera capabilities and intelligent flight modes, making it an ideal choice for both casual users and enthusiasts.

Enhanced Battery
Life

Battery

The DJI Mini 4 Pro features an upgraded battery that allows for up to 46 minutes of flight time, which is an increase of around 12 minutes compared to the 34-minute maximum flight time of the Mini 3 Pro. This improvement in battery life enhances the drone's efficiency, offering longer flight sessions without sacrificing portability.

4K/60fps HDR True Vertical Shooting

Camera

Sensor: 1/1.3-inch CMOS Resolution: 48 MP photos
Video: 4K/60fps HDR, 1080p/100fps slow motion
Aperture: f/1.7 for low-light performance
Zoom: 4x digital zoom
Shooting Modes: True Vertical Shooting, SmartPhoto, Panorama
Stabilization: 3-axis gimbal for smooth footage
Transmission Range: Up to 12 km with O4 video transmission.

Active Track 360°

Active Track 360°

The ActiveTrack 360° feature on the DJI Mini 4 Pro enhances the standard ActiveTrack by enabling full 360-degree tracking around a subject. This allows the drone to move seamlessly around the subject in a circular path while keeping it centered, resulting in immersive, cinematic shots from every angle
